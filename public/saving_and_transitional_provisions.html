Regulation 17

SCHEDULESaving and transitional provisions

This schedule has no associated Policy Notes

The Best Start Grants Regulations 2018

1.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s application for assistance was made on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 3, paragraphs 6 and 7 of schedule 2 of the Best Start Grants Regulations 2018 continue to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as they did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the amount of assistance to which the individual is entitled.

(3) Despite regulation 3, paragraph 4 of schedule 3 and paragraph 5 of schedule 4 of the Best Start Grants Regulations 2018 continue to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as they did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the value of assistance to which the individual is entitled.

The Best Start Foods Regulations 2019

2.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s entitlement to benefit includes an award for the period on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 4, regulation 13 of the Best Start Foods Regulations 2019 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mention in sub-paragraph (1).

The Funeral Support Payment Regulations 2019

3.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s application for assistance was made on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 5, regulation 13 of the Funeral Support Payment Regulations 2019 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the amount of assistance to which the individual is entitled.

The Young Carer Grants Regulations 2019

4.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s application for assistance was made on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 6, regulation 12 of the Young Carer Grants Regulations 2019 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the amount of assistance to which the individual is entitled.

The Child Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2020

5.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual is entitled to be paid child winter heating assistance in respect of a qualifying week occurring on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 7, regulation 10 of the Child Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2020 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the value of assistance to which the individual is entitled in respect of the qualifying week mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

(3) In this paragraph, “child winter heating assistance” and “qualifying week” have the meanings given in regulation 2 of the Child Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2020.

The Scottish Child Payment Regulations 2020

6.—(1) Subject to sub-paragraph (3), sub-paragraph (2) applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 8, regulation 20(1) of the Scottish Child Payment Regulations 2020 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

(3) Where, in accordance with regulation 21 of the Scottish Child Payment Regulations 2020, a payment is to be made in respect of a week beginning with a day falling before 1 April 2025 and ending with a day falling on or after that date, sub-paragraph (2) does not apply.

(4) In sub-paragraph (3), “week” has the same meaning as in regulation 8 of the Scottish Child Payment Regulations 2020.

The Child Disability Payment Regulations 2021

7.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 8 April 2024 and before 7 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 9, regulation 23 of the Child Disability Payment Regulations 2021 continues to have effect on and after 7 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

The Adult Disability Payment Regulations 2022

8.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 8 April 2024 and before 7 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 10, regulation 34 of the Adult Disability Payment Regulations 2022 continues to have effect on and after 7 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

The Adult Disability Payment Transitional Regulations 2022

9.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 8 April 2024 and before 7 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 11, regulation 6(1) of the Adult Disability Payment Transitional Regulations 2022 continues to have effect on and after 7 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

The Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2023

10.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual is entitled to be paid winter heating payment in respect of a qualifying week occurring on or after 1 April 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 12, regulation 9(1) of the Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2023 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the value of assistance to which the individual is entitled in respect of the qualifying week mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

(3) In this paragraph, “winter heating payment” and “qualifying week” have the meanings given in regulation 2 of the Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2023.

The Carer Support Payment Regulations 2023

11.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 7 April 2024 and before 6 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 13(2) and (3), regulations 14(1) and 16(1) of the Carer Support Payment Regulations 2023 continue to have effect on and after 6 April 2025 as they did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

The Pension Age Disability Payment Regulations 2024

12.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 21 October 2024 and before 7 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 14, regulation 26(1) of the Pension Age Disability Payment Regulations 2024 continues to have effect on and after 7 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

The Pension Age Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2024

13.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual is entitled to be paid Pension Age Winter Heating Payment in respect of a qualifying week occurring on or after 20 November 2024 and before 1 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 15, regulation 10 of the Pension Age Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2024 continues to have effect on and after 1 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date for the purpose of determining the amount of assistance to which the individual is entitled.

(3) In this paragraph, “Pension Age Winter Heating Payment” and “qualifying week” have the meanings given in regulation 2 of the Pension Age Winter Heating Payment Regulations 2024.

The Scottish Adult Disability Living Allowance Regulations 2025

14.—(1) This paragraph applies where an individual’s determination of entitlement includes an award for the period on or after 21 March 2025 and before 7 April 2025.

(2) Despite regulation 16(3), regulation 29 of the Scottish Adult Disability Living Allowance Regulations 2025 continues to have effect on and after 7 April 2025 as it did immediately before that date in respect of the period mentioned in sub-paragraph (1).

Draft Policy Note

Draft Policy Note sets out a brief statement of the purpose of a Draft Scottish Statutory Instrument and provides information about its policy objective and policy implications. They aim to make the Draft Scottish Statutory Instrument accessible to readers who are not legally qualified and accompany any Scottish Statutory Instrument or Draft Scottish Statutory Instrument laid before the Scottish Parliament from July 2012 onwards. Prior to this date these type of notes existed as 'Executive Notes' and accompanied Draft Scottish Statutory Instruments from July 2005 until July 2012.
